--Overview of Automated Test Cases--


Login Test:
•Automates the login functionality by navigating to the login page, entering valid credentials, and verifying successful login and then logout.
•Tools and Methods: page.goto, page.fill, page.click, and assertions using expect.

Navigation Test:
•Verifies that the "Terms of Use" and "Privacy Policy" links in the footer correctly navigate to their respective pages.
•Includes steps to scroll to the footer, click the links, and validate the page URLs.
•Tools and Methods: page.locator, page.click, and expect.

Search Functionality Test:
•Checks the search functionality by entering specific keywords like "Computer Science", submitting the search form, and verifying that the results page is displayed with relevant content.
•Tools and Methods: page.fill, page.click, expect.
